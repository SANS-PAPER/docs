## 🚀 Testing Release 1st Auguest 2025

### 🔹 Create Jobs
#### 🧪 What's new
- Admin Can create Jobs.
  
## Steps:

- Login to the account using credential 
- Switch to admin role
- Go to History
- Click the plus button in the right top 
- Insert the details according to job and click on the create button
- The new job will update in History "All" section

## 🚀 Testing Release 11th July 2025

### 🔹 Fix Login Issue  
#### 🧪 What's new
- After first time user has successfully sign up into Sans Paper ID, they cannot sign in into the web app.
- This login issue has been solved by adding new user data into our database.  

## Steps:
- Open Sans Paper ID web on a browser.
- Click on the Sign In button.
- Click on the Sign Up button.
<img
    src="/images/spiweb/signup auth0.png"
    alt="signup"
  />

- Enter your email address and password.
- Click on the "Continue" button.
- Log out from the web app.
- Sign In into the web app again.


### 🔹 User Registration form  
#### 🧪 What's new
- After user has sign up into the web app, user-registration form will popup in the screen for the user to complete their profile details. 

## Steps:
- After Sign Up, User Registration form will popup on the screen.
- User need to fill up all the details in the form.
<img
    src="/images/spiweb/user-registration.png"
    alt="user-registration"
  />
- Click on the save button.
- If user do not want to complete the profile details yet, they can choose to close the popup, however, the popup will show again until they have complete their profile.
