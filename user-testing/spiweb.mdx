### 🔹 Friction Point Identification

Ranked by how likely each step is to cause a drop-off, based on whether it depends on external systems (email, AI processing), requires manual data entry, or gives the user no visible confirmation or recovery path.

| # | Step in Flow | Type of Friction | Risk to Sign-Up Completion | Recommendation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Email verification after "Claim Profile" | External dependency (email delivery) | High — user must leave the app; delayed, filtered, or spam-folder emails stall or lose the user entirely | Add in-app resend + spam-folder reminder; consider allowing profile use before verification with a visible "unverified" banner |
| 2 | User Registration Form (post sign-up) | Manual data entry, recurring popup | Medium — the form reappears every session until fully completed, which reads as a blocker/loop rather than a one-time step | Allow "Save & continue later" with a persistent but dismissible reminder instead of a forced popup |
| 3 | Organization Summary — "Edit with AI" | AI processing step (Business Title, Years of Experience, Skills/Highlights required before "Generate Summary" works) | High — this is the exact step that blocked today's fresh sign-up; if required fields are unclear or the AI call fails/times out, the user has no forward path | Make this step explicitly optional/skippable on first login; add a visible loading state, error/retry messaging, and a manual-entry fallback if AI generation fails |
| 4 | Auto-created Organization (on sign-up) | Silent system action, no confirmation shown | Low–Medium — user may not realize an org was created for them and may try to create a duplicate | Add a one-line confirmation toast: "Your organization '\<name\>' was created automatically" |
| 5 | "Add Skills" → external Sans Paper Form | Context switch to a separate form/tab | Medium — leaving the core app to fill an external form is a natural drop-off point | Track completion/return rate separately; consider embedding the form instead of opening a new tab |
| 6 | Role confusion (Admin / Super Admin / Employee) | Manual configuration, no guided default | Medium — new users are not told which role they need before they can create or apply for jobs | Add a short role-selection prompt during onboarding with plain-language descriptions |

<Warning>
  Step 10 (Organization Summary — Edit with AI) is the confirmed live blocker from today's fresh registration attempt. This should be the first item fixed or made skippable, since it currently sits between a new user and their active dashboard with no visible fallback.
</Warning>

### 🔹 Client-Facing SOP (Simplified)

A short, plain-language version of the walkthrough for client / BMS documentation. Internal jargon and friction-point commentary are omitted here — those live in the friction table above for the internal team only.

**Sans Paper ID — Getting Started for New Users**

1. Go to the Sans Paper ID Web sign-up page and click **Sign Up**.
2. Enter a work email address and create a password, then click **Continue**.
3. Sign in with the new account.
4. Complete the short registration form when it appears (name, role details). This can be saved partially, but it will reappear until finished.
5. Your organization is created automatically — no separate action needed.
6. On your Profile page, click **Claim Profile**, then check your email inbox (and spam folder) for the verification link.
7. Click the verification link to confirm your profile.
8. On the Organization page, click **Edit with AI**, fill in your business title, years of experience, and key skills, then click **Generate Summary**. If this step fails to load, refresh once and retry, or fill the summary in manually and continue.
9. (Optional) Click **Add Skills** to open the skills form and add your certifications.
10. You're in — your dashboard is now active and you can search or post jobs.

<Note>
  Once Step 10 (Edit with AI) is stabilized, this SOP can go to clients as-is.
</Note>
