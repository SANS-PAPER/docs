## Steps:
- Open Sans Paper Connect Web on a browser.
- Click on the Login button.
<img
    src="/images/spc/login-spc.png"
    alt="signin"
  />
- Choose your login preference.
- If user enter email address and password, just click on the "Continue' button.
- If user choose other Sign In options, follow the steps until successfully Sign In.
<img
    src="/images/sphweb/othersignin.png"
    alt="signup"
  />

### Set Organization Settings
- Go to the top right bar, click on the down arrow button and select `Organization Settings`.
- Fill in Upvise account username and password.
- Select which Upvise server.
- Click on the refresh button to generate an API key.
- Click save to save the changes.
<img
    src="/images/spc/setup-upvise-account.png"
    alt="signup"
  />

### Upvise Export and Import Contacts
- On the left menu bar, click on `Upvise` and a dropdown menu will appear. Click on `Export Contacts`.
- To start, click on the `Get Contacts` button, and 2 lists will appear.

<Note>
  Please note that only Upvise contacts with email is shown, because Upvise contacts without email cannot be imported into Sans Paper database.
</Note>

- The first list is the option to Export Sans Paper Contacts to Upvise. Click on the checkbox to select a contact and click `Export Selected Contacts to Upvise` button to start exporting.
<img
    src="/images/spc/contacts-sp-upvise.png"
  />

- The second list is the option to Export Upvise Contacts to Sans Paper. Click on the checkbox to select a contact and click `Export Selected Contacts to Sans Paper` button to start exporting.
<img
    src="/images/spc/contacts-upvise-sp.png"
  />

- After completing the export, the page will remove the tickboxes if the contacts already exists in each list.
