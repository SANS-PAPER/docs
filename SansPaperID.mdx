## Introduction
This app is a **powerful profile-building platform** designed to showcase your skills, work experiences, and more in a **professional and engaging way**. 

With built-in **real-time chat features** and an innovative **map-based discovery**, it enables seamless communication and job searching for professionals and recruiters alike. Here's everything you need to know to get started!

---

## Getting Started

### Installation
Follow these steps to install :

#### Mobile:
1. Download the app from the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.sanspaper.spi&pcampaignid=web_share) or [Apple App Store](#).
2. Install the app on your device.
3. Sign up using your email address or preferred social login.

#### Web:
1. Visit the [official website](https://id.sanspaper.com/).
2. Create an account or log in with your existing credentials.

### Quick Start
1. Complete your profile by adding **Name**, **Job Title**, and **Skills**.
2. Upload a professional profile picture.
3. Explore the **Dashboard** to connect with others and showcase your portfolio.
4. Use the **Search** feature to find colleagues, professionals, or potential employers.

## FAQ

### How can I reset my password?
- Go to the **Login Page** and click on "Forgot Password." Follow the instructions sent to your email.

### Can I export my profile as a PDF?
- Yes! Go to your **Profile Page** and click "Export" to download your profile as a PDF.

### Is there a subscription fee?
- The app is free to use, but premium features are available with a subscription.
