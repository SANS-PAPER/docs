## Products

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card
    title="Sans Paper Form"
    icon="paper-plane"
    href="/spform"
  >
    Sans Paper Form is an advanced form builder that empowers users to create dynamic, professional forms in minutes. With its cutting-edge AI image tagging tools, it simplifies data organization and enhances productivity, making it the perfect solution for businesses, event managers, and creatives. Try it today to experience seamless form creation with AI-powered efficiency.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Sans Paper ID"
    icon="paper-plane"
    href="/spID"
  >
    Sans Paper ID is a powerful profile builder app designed to showcase your skills, work experiences, and more in a professional and engaging way. With built-in real-time chat features, it enables seamless communication with colleagues, managers, and potential employers. Employers can effortlessly discover ideal candidates through skill-based profiles, making it a must-have tool for professionals and recruiters alike.
  </Card>
   <Card
    title="Sans Paper Hub"
    icon="paper-plane"
    href="/sphub"
  >
    Sans Paper Hub is a pool of business platforms stitched together to facilitate ease of access. You can literally manage all your best-of-breed construction business platforms in one location. You can also keep track of your users, your costs, your support requests, integrate platforms with ease and extend the benefits of your platforms to achieve more on Platform hub. 
Among the finest platforms arrayed, the best of breed platforms currently available include Upvise, Sans Paper, G Suite, 365, Xero, Myki, MYOB Advanced, Acumatica, and Workbench. 
Therefore, you can get expert help when you need it, raising support tickets and have the option to choose a support plan that best fits your business and alongside, easily monitor your account billing and make secure automated payments.

  </Card>
   <Card
    title="Sans Paper Connect"
    icon="paper-plane"
    href="/spconnect"
  >
    Sans Paper Connect is a platform that connects your business platforms to mobile forms. Do you have a platform in your business operations that is keeping you bound to a desk? Sans Paper Connect, connects to your ERP, CRM or other web-based platforms to make it mobile, connects your field teams with the office and provides you with more data streams so you can stay across all your business activities. Sans Paper Connect extends the value of your existing platforms. Sans Paper Connect currently works with Upvise and allows for better and faster delivery of information from the field to relevant teams – without the need to even step foot outside the door. Literally meaning “without paper”, Sans Paper empowers your workers to be your eyes in the field, sharing information that can help identify problems and faults related to equipment or optimise and improve your processes. Sans Paper Connect is an intuitive, light-weight app that lets your busy users do what they need quickly. It takes the power of process to your field workers, allowing the whole team – from managers to those on the tools – to benefit from digitisation.
  </Card>
