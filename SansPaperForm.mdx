## Introduction

Sans Paper Form is an advanced form builder that empowers users to create dynamic, professional forms in minutes. With its cutting-edge AI image tagging tools, it simplifies data organization and enhances productivity, making it the perfect solution for businesses, event managers, and creatives. Try it today to experience seamless form creation with AI-powered efficiency.

## Getting Started

### Installation
Follow these steps to install :

#### Mobile:
1. Download the app from the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.sanspaper.spi&pcampaignid=web_share) or [Apple App Store](#).

#### Web:
1. Visit the [official website](https://id.sanspaper.com/).
2. Create an account or log in with your existing credentials.

## Mobile

### Quick Start
The app consists of three main screens: **Profile**, **Forms**, and **History**.

#### Profile
This section displays your name, the current app version, and provides key functionalities such as:
- Sending invites
- Accessing the privacy policy
- Logging out

#### Forms
In this tab, you will find:
- Forms you have created and own
- Forms shared with you by other organizations

This section allows you to efficiently manage and access your forms.

#### History
The History tab keeps track of all your submitted forms. If you have a **Manager** or **Admin** role, you will also see:
- **Approve** and **Reject** buttons to take action on submitted forms

This streamlined navigation ensures a smooth user experience, making it easy to manage profiles, forms, and submissions efficiently.

#### **How to Create a Subform**

Follow these steps to set up a subform:

###### **Step 1: Setup on the Web**
1. Log in to your account at [SansPaper Forms](https://form.sanspaper.com/).
2. Navigate to the **Forms** tab in the left sidebar menu.
3. Click on **Form Templates**.
4. Select a form where you want to set up the subform.
5. Click the arrow next to the form and select **Edit**.
6. Choose the field where you want to set up the subform.
7. Click the **Properties** icon.
8. Go to the **Event** tab and click the **Add New** button.
9. Fill in the required details:
   - **Name**: Enter a name for the event.
   - **Effects**: Select **Open Subform** (currently the only working effect).
10. Complete all the fields in the **Settings** section.
11. Click **Save**.

---

###### **Step 2: Testing on Mobile**
1. Open the **SPForm** mobile app.
2. Select the form where you created the subform event.
3. Enter the value that you set in the designated field.
4. A pop-up should appear, informing you that a subform needs to be filled in at the end of the current form.
5. Submit the current form.
6. The app will navigate you to the subform.
7. Fill in the subform and submit it.
8. You can verify submission by checking the **History** tab, where two forms should now be recorded.

**Note:** If the subform is not submitted, the first form will not be submitted either.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues
1. **Unable to Log In:**
   - Ensure your internet connection is stable.
   - Reset your password if needed.

2. **Profile Not Updating:**
   - Check for app updates.
   - Clear the app cache in your device settings.
